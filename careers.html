Careers

Beginning July 9, 2012, MARTA has transitioned to a fully automated recruiting system, ‘iRecruitment’. This new system enables applicants to easily find and apply for jobs at MARTA, to maintain and update their online profile or resume, or to check the status of job applications, as well as receive email notifications related to the status of their application.

You may view available job opportunities without logging in, but must log in for anything additional.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

You may conduct a job search without being registered or logging in.  To submit an application, however, you must be registered and logged in.  If you have never been a MARTA employee and are not already registered, you may access registration from the home page by clicking the ‘Register today’ button.

If you are a current employee of MARTA or an external candidate who has applied to MARTA before in the old system or at least registered in the old system after 1st Jun 2012, you have been mailed or emailed your login credentials and do not need to register. 

If you are not a current MARTA employee, your username is the email address you entered in your registration. If you are a current MARTA employee, your username and password are your Oracle Applications user name and password.  If you did not already have an Oracle Applications account when we converted to iRecruitment, your login credentials were sent to you by mail if you are represented or by email if you are non-represented.  Your username is probably the same as your MARTA network login name, but your password is probably not unless you changed it to that.

If you’re copying the password and pasting it in, please be careful not to copy a leading or trailing blank space. If you’re typing it in, please watch for numbers that could be mistaken for alphabets (0 and O) and vice-versa. If you've failed to login 3 times in a row, you will need to click on "Forgot Password" again to unlock your account.

You can view the status of your application when you login to your account. Under “Jobs Applied For” section, you can view all active job applications and the status should indicate “Active Application”, “Interview”, “Offer” and so on. If you are selected for an interview, Human Resources or the hiring department will contact you and there is no follow up expected from applicants after submitting their applications. MARTA’s Service Desk can only assist in resolving technical problems with the system and will not be able to answer questions regarding the status of applications or other career advice.

For the speediest resolution, please ensure you go over all the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) thoroughly.  We also strongly advise you to go through the appropriate video tutorial that can be accessed from the iRecruitment page or the top of this page.  If your problem remains unresolved after going through the FAQs and the video tutorial, you may contact MARTA’s Service Desk at 404-848-5888.Please remember that the Service Desk can only assist in resolving technical problems with the system and will not be able to answer questions regarding the status of applications or the overall activities within Recruiting.

MARTA conducts background checks on all external applicants upon acceptance of a contingent job offer.  We currently use a third party vendor to conduct the background checks.  Internal applicants must also have a background check if one was not completed one year prior to accepting a new position.  Background checks will be performed in compliance with the Fair Credit Reporting Act.
