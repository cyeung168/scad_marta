University Pass

Students: $68.50
Faculty/Staff: $83.80

MARTA's University Pass Program (UPASS) partners with colleges, universities and technical/vocational schools shown below to sell discounted MARTA Breeze Passes to its students and faculty/staff.

If you are a student, parent of a college student or faculty/staff member, you will not be able to obtain a pass directly from MARTA.

The UPASS is not sold at ridestores or online.

If your university or college is listed on the right, please contact your school to find out exactly where they have chosen to sell the passes; some departments to try are Student Services, Student Accounts or Bookstore.

Only school administrators may request account setup; if you are not a school administrator, please feel free to share contact information below.

The Breeze UPASS is good for unlimited trips on MARTA trains and buses all day and every day of the week for the calendar month (first day of the month to the last day of the month.)

With your UPASS,  you may transfer onto other regional bus services (CCT, Xpress or Gwinnett) and may be required additional fare; but you must pay fare for trips originating on other regional bus services and conversely transfer at no cost onto MARTA bus or rail.  You must purchase a separate Breeze Card to load other fares if needed to pay for fare originating on regional bus services.
