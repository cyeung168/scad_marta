Breeze Card

No more transfers. One card is all you need for Convenience, Reliability, and Security.

Learn how to purchase your Breeze Card by visiting: breezecard.com

Find out how to add value to a Breeze Card!

A Breeze Card is the fastest, easiest and most convenient way to ride MARTA. With Breeze, there are no tokens to fumble with, no worries about exact change and no danger of getting your card stuck in the reader. Just tap and go.

Now you can store your discounted weekly or monthly pass on one card, as well as load up to $300 cash value. Never worry about a lost or stolen Breeze Card when you sign up for Balance Protection. Breeze’s Balance Protection enables us to issue you a new card with the balance remaining on it at the time of notification. We will cancel your old card so no one can use it (you will be charged a $1 fee for the replacement card).  Reload your card at any Breeze Vending Machine or MARTA Ride Store. Coming Soon to a computer near you: reload online with credit/debit.

Simply:

Tap your Breeze Card to the reader located on the station entry gate or bus fare box.
The reader will let you know your card has been accepted and flash the remaining balance on the screen.
Breeze will open the entry gate for you or signal the bus driver that you have paid.
On buses, transfers are automatically loaded. At rail stations, tap your Breeze Card on the Breeze target (blue, circular card reader)

How to Add Value

Adding Value to Your Breeze Card is easy and you can use one of two options.

Breeze Vending Machine

Tap your Breeze Card  on the Breeze target.
Select the value you would like to place on your card by pressing the corresponding button(s) on the menu screen.
Insert payment into the appropriate slot - cash, coins or debit/credit card.
Tap your Breeze Card on the card reader a second time to load the value onto your card.

Breeze Bus Fare Box

Tap Breeze Card on the bus farebox, insert cash (bills and/or coins) and tap your card again to load the value and transfer.
