Visitor Pass

Planning a meeting, convention or event can be tedious and time consuming.  Buying a Day Pass for yourself, family or medium to large group will eliminate the hassle of traffic and expensive parking.  The Day Pass provides unlimited travel for consecutive calendar days on MARTA bus and rail and is an easy and inexpensive way to travel.

Travel effortlessly from Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport to downtown Atlanta, Buckhead, Decatur, the Georgia World Congress Center, Phillips Arena, Georgia Aquarium, High Museum of Art, Lenox Mall and many shops, restaurants and area attractions.  Not only does MARTA get your attendees to your event, meeting or convention but we help you experience all the great qualities of Atlanta.

A one-time $1 surcharge for cards.
All passes are available at the Breeze Vending Machines at all 38 rail stations, including the airport rail station.

All Breeze Vending Machines accept major credit cards, debit cards and cash and will supply a receipt if requested during transaction. At the station vending machines one (1) plastic card may be purchased per transaction.

Please call in your order if any one of the following conditions apply:

1. You are ordering between eleven and fourteen passes on one credit card.

2. Your advance order is being covered by American Express,

A minimum of 14 business days is required to process and ship advance orders
