Parking

MARTA offers over 24,000 FREE parking spaces at many of our stations. Daily parking (less than 24 hours) is available for free at 23 MARTA stations. Long-term parking is available at Brookhaven, Dunwoody, Kensington, Lenox and Sandy Springs Stations for $5/day, or at College Park, Doraville, Lindbergh and North Springs stations for $8/day. MARTA also offers Park & Ride locations at Windward Parkway, Mansell Road, Barge Road and South Fulton.

  

Parking Procedures

All customers using parking decks must follow the procedures below to receive FREE Daily Parking (less than 24 hours

1.  Pull ticket from dispenser upon entering the parking deck
2 . Park only in designated MARTA parking areas
3 . Take parking ticket and validate it on your return trip to the station after riding MARTA (validation machines are located on the faregate level inside of the station area)
4.  Customers have 30 minutes to exit the parking deck after validating ticket, otherwise you will be charged the posted daily rate.

Payment Options

MARTA has made it easier for customers to park anywhere in the system by accepting credit cards at all parking facilities. MARTA accepts VIsa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express at all long-term parking locations.  Checks are not accepted. For additional information, please call Customer Service at 404-848-5000.
