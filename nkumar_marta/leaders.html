Leadership

For over a quarter of a century, MARTA has moved over 3.5 billion people throughout Atlanta and the surrounding cities.  We have been a staple to the community and a great economic driver for the city.  Today, we are one of the top 10 transportation agencies in the United States and we are striving to be bigger and better.  While our opportunities for growth are eminent, our commitment to our customers and our communities in which we operate is even greater.

Our team of dedicated leaders are here to move us into the next generation.  They are here to lead our organization, to help us grow, to help our communities thrive and to become a sustainable as well as profitable company for the next generation of riders.

Executive Management Team

Beverly A. Scott, Ph.D.
General Manager/Chief Executive Officer

In October 2007, Beverly A. Scott, Ph.D. became General Manager and Chief Executive Officer of the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority (MARTA), the ninth largest transit system in the United States. Recognized throughout the industry as a progressive leader of exceptional skill and foresight, Dr. Scott brings strong vision and leadership to MARTA’s ongoing implementation of improvements and its efforts to develop a comprehensive regional transportation plan. Dwight A. Ferrell
Dep General Manager/Chief Operating Officier

Dwight A. Ferrell is the Deputy General Manager & Chief Operating Officer (DGM/COO) for the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority (MARTA), returning to the ninth largest transit system in North America where he served as Senior Vice President/COO from 1999 to 2001. Ferrell is responsible for the oversight of the Authority’s day-to-day operations with the objective of providing efficient, safe and reliable transit service to the public. His core responsibilities include the direction and oversight of the Authority's Operations, Maintenance, Infrastructure, Police Services, Safety, Mobility Services, Labor Relations, Quality Assurance & Configuration Management, and Technical Training departments. Ferrell’s duties also include building and maintaining relationships with the Authority’s external stakeholders including both government entities and the business community. Read more...

Ted Basta
Chief of Business Support Services

Theodore J. Basta, Jr., is the Chief of Business Support Services at MARTA. In this capacity, he serves as a key member of the Executive Management Team providing direction and oversight to all business units for the Authority. Mr. Basta directly oversees the activities of the Budget, Finance & Accounting; Contracts & Procurement; Human Resources, Labor Relations, Training, DEO, Employee Availability; Information Technology; Research & Development; and the Administrative Services’ Divisions.
